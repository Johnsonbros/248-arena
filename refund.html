Free trial

Every new subscription starts with a 7-day free trial. You are not charged during the trial, and if you cancel before it ends you pay nothing at all. This is the best way to make sure 248 Arena is right for you before spending a dollar.

Monthly subscriptions

Because you get a full week free to evaluate the app, monthly charges are generally non-refundable once billed. You can cancel anytime to stop future charges; cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period and you keep access until then.

Annual subscriptions

If you purchased an annual plan, you may request a refund within 14 days of the initial charge for a full refund, provided the request is made in good faith. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable but can be canceled to prevent renewal.

How to cancel or request a refund

Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal, or email krazedrecords@gmail.com and we'll take care of it. For refund requests, include the email used at checkout.

Exceptional cases

If you were charged in error or experienced a technical problem that prevented you from using the service, contact us — we'll make it right.
